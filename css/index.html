Who We Are

Rift Raff was started by a former rock & roll musician that saw the need to help small music stores from going out of business. And in the process, he discovered a way to help music enthusiasts find the guitars and instruments they always wanted but didn’t know where to go. Today we help buyers connect with sellers from all over the world. With thousands of guitars and musical instruments to choose from, the music sales industry will never be the same again.
